The Importance of Hero Selection

In order to understand team composition, hero selection must be taken into consideration.

We here at Overwatchers Anonymous believe in one, and only one, strategy for hero selection:

DON'T PICK SOMEONE YOU'VE NEVER TRAINED WITH BEFORE!!!!!!!!!!!!

This will encourage you to go through practice and arcade modes which, unlike quickplay and competitive, will keep your integrity and pride in check.

However, if you choose to ignore us, go ahead! We certainly won't judge.....at least until your entire squad gets demoted to silver.

But seriously, don't say we didn't warn you!
